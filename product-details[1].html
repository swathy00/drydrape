DryDrape: Smart Drying for Delicates

DryDrape is a next-generation clothes hanger system specifically designed for delicate, small, and travel garments. Combining active airflow technology and a compact foldable design, DryDrape revolutionizes the way you dry clothes at home or on the go.

Fast & Gentle Drying

DryDrape uses built-in air channels and optional fan-assisted airflow to reduce drying time by up to 70%, while protecting fabric texture, elasticity, and color.

Eco-Friendly & Energy Efficient

Skip the tumble dryer. DryDrape uses minimal electricity or no electricity at all when used passively. A great way to save energy and reduce your carbon footprint.

Compact, Foldable & Travel-Friendly

Designed to fold flat and fit in backpacks, carry-ons, or desk drawers, DryDrape is perfect for frequent travelers, apartment dwellers, college students, and minimalists.
